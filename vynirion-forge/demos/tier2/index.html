Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a full roof replacement take?
Most residential replacements take 1–2 days depending on the size and complexity of the roof. We'll give you a specific timeline in your estimate.
Do you work with insurance companies?
Yes. We have experience with all major insurers and can help you navigate the claims process, document damage, and coordinate with your adjuster.
What kind of warranty do you offer?
We back all replacement work with a 10-year workmanship warranty in addition to the manufacturer's warranty on materials (typically 25–50 years for asphalt shingles).
How do I know if I need a repair or a full replacement?
That's exactly what our free inspection is for. We'll give you an honest recommendation — if a repair is enough, we'll tell you. We don't push replacements when they're not warranted.
