Our Story

From One Truck to St. Louis's Most Trusted Crew

Apex started in 2006 with one truck, two ladders, and a founder who'd spent a decade watching other companies cut corners. The rule from day one: install every roof like it's going on your own mother's house.

Twenty years and 1,200 roofs later, that rule hasn't changed. We've grown into one of the region's few GAF Master Elite contractors, built a commercial division, and assembled crews that other companies try to poach every season. But every project still gets the same treatment as roof number one.
